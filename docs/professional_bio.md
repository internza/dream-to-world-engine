# Professional Biographies

## Zyad Alkurdi
Zyad is a Computer Science student at the University of Cincinnati with strong experience in full stack development through two internships at Siemens Digital Industries Software. His work spans React, TypeScript, Java, REST services, SQL, and large scale enterprise platforms. He has contributed to multiple production releases and feature sets across the Value Stream and Digital Thread ecosystem.

He is focused on building intelligent systems that connect AI, visualization, and interactive design. For the Dream to World Engine project, Zyad leads software architecture, system design, and real time scene rendering. His interests include graphics programming, three dimensional environments, automation, performance engineering, and creative AI tools. He aims to continue working in software engineering after graduation with a long term goal of building advanced visual and generative systems.
